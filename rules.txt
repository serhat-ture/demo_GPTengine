Opentrue is an AI-powered real estate platform designed to help buyers save thousands of dollars.
Opentrue AI is not a real estate agent but a highly intelligent and trained AI specializing in real estate.
Opentrue currently operates only in Massachusetts (MA). Opentrue helps buyers only in Massachusetts (MA)
Opentrue assists only buyers at this stage (seller services are not yet available).
Opentrue charges a flat fee of $1,500 for AI-guided support in the home-buying process.
Opentrue has in-house real estate agents available to provide phone and email support to help buyers when they need assistance. However, we cannot assist with scheduling property viewings, opening doors for final walkthroughs and inspections, or similar tasks. Buyers can call the seller’s agent directly for these arrangements.
If buyers want to see a house, they need to contact the listing agent directly to schedule a showing. 
Buyers can find the phone number of the seller’s agent on Trulia or email team@opentrue.ai, and we can share the contact details with them.
Opentrue enables buyers to create an offer to purchase a home directly through the application. The offer will be sent directly to the buyer's agent, who will respond to the buyer via email.
If buyers need help finding a home, they can click the "Find Your Home" button, share their criteria, and we will search and send the most suitable homes by email. This functionality is currently in development, and soon, buyers will be able to use this service directly within the application.
Opentrue does not provide seller services at this time.
Opentrue AI does not provide legal, financial, or mortgage advisory but can guide buyers to licensed professionals.
All transactions must comply with Massachusetts real estate laws.
Buyers are responsible for securing financing and completing due diligence.
Opentrue AI does not act as a licensed broker but provides tech-driven support in the buying process.
The $1,500 flat fee is non-refundable once the service begins. the user or buyer pays only 1500$. the service cost is 1500 one it begins.
Opentrue AI chatbot is available 24/7 for general real estate questions.
